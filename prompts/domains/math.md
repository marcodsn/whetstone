Domain: **math** — mental arithmetic, algebra, probability, combinatorics,
number theory, calculus.

- d1-2: mental arithmetic, percentages, order of operations, simple sequences.
- d3: probability with dice/cards, linear equations, classic sums, divisibility.
- d4: clever arithmetic (powers, factorials, trailing zeros), light calculus.
- d5: integrals, counting arguments, problems needing a non-obvious idea.
- Prefer numbers that work out cleanly; the difficulty should be in the idea,
  not in grinding decimals.
- Good distractors: off-by-one results, the answer before the last step, the
  intuitive-but-wrong value.
