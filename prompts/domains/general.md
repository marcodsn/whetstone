Domain: **general** — knowledge with a twist, estimation, classic paradoxes.

- Prefer questions where the obvious answer is wrong but the truth is
  verifiable: misattributed quotes, surprising geography, naming traps
  (Panama hats, Hundred Years' War).
- Order-of-magnitude estimation as `choice` (googol vs atoms, scale
  comparisons).
- Classic probability paradoxes (Monty Hall, birthday) only if not already
  present.
- Wordplay riddles as `input` when the answer is a single word.
- Avoid trivia that is mere memorization with no twist or insight, and avoid
  anything whose answer might have changed recently (population counts,
  record holders, current events).
